Weather app

The weather app I created is a simple and easy-to-use tool for checking the current weather conditions and forecast for a given location. The app displays the temperature in Celsius and Fahrenheit, as well as the humidity, wind speed, and weather icon. Users can enter the name of a city or town in the search bar, and the app will display the corresponding weather information.
